In plain English

Record the introduction before Discord registration exists.

A guild member can report that they recruited a new character within the allowed reporting window. The entry can remain Awaiting registration for as long as necessary.

When that character later registers to a genuine new Discord account, Luminox can move the report to staff review. Recruiters can claim ownership, other members can dispute it and authorized staff issue the final verdict.
