In plain English

Members stream. Luminox handles the stopwatch.

When an eligible member starts sharing their screen in a configured voice channel, Luminox opens a lightweight session. A stream must last at least ten minutes before it qualifies.

After qualification, completed minutes are rewarded proportionally. The system does not need to write a database entry every second, and daily limits protect the guild from accidental or abusive endless sessions.
