Eligibility

Do I need a character on Secura?

Lumina is a Tibia guild on Secura. Full guild membership requires an accepted Lumina character on Secura. The Discord server can allow limited guest access for eligible registered characters outside the guild.

Is there one fixed level requirement?

This public site does not promise a permanent level threshold. Staff can evaluate the current needs of the guild, your character and whether your attitude matches the community.

Can I join with friends?

You can mention friends and shared plans in your application, but every account and character must be evaluated and registered honestly.

Registration

Why is Discord registration mandatory?

Registration proves character ownership through a temporary Tibia.com comment code. It connects your Discord account, characters, main and current guild rank without asking for Tibia credentials.

What happens if verification does not finish?

Your saved timezone remains available. Use the Registration panel again with the same character name after checking the exact spelling and placing the code at the beginning of the character comment.

Can I register more than one character?

Yes, subject to the server limits. One character becomes your main and your additional registered characters remain connected to the same Discord account.

Community life

What does Lumina organize?

Members can take part in hunts, bosses, quests, general events, streams, roleplay, guild progression and everyday Discord conversation.

Do I need to be active every day?

Lumina values reliable participation, not constant presence. Communicate when commitments change and contribute in a way that is realistic for your schedule.

Which language does the guild use?

Lumina is international. Structured panels, clear written information and Discord timestamps help members from different countries coordinate.

Application

How do I apply?

Read the current rules, follow the joining instructions and use the available public application or private support route published in Discord.

Can I ask questions privately first?

Yes. Open a ticket through the Support panel for registration questions, sensitive concerns or help choosing the correct route.

Does applying guarantee acceptance?

No. An application starts a review. Staff can consider guild fit, identity, conduct and the current needs of the community before deciding.
