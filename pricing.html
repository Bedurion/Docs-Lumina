Private flagship

Founder Edition

Invite only

The complete, fastest-moving Luminox environment built specifically around Lumina's operations.

  • Full Tibia identity, economy, events and intelligence stack.
  • Founder refresh cadence and unrestricted internal iteration.
  • Early access to new systems before public product packaging.
  • Configuration bound to the authorized Founder server.
  • Indefinite ticket metadata retention where appropriate.

Tibia guilds

Community Edition

Free + Premiumplanned

The public Tibia-focused edition for guilds that need verified characters, world-aware systems and organized Discord operations.

  • Character registration, guild verification and rank mapping.
  • Panel-first events, support, Finder and GuildBank workflows.
  • Watchlists, progression and Tibia-aware intelligence modules.
  • Free entry path with bounded retention and standard service.
  • Premium path planned for expanded limits, retention and support.

Beyond one game

Universal Edition

Free + Premiumplanned

A game-agnostic edition that keeps Luminox's strongest guild operations without depending on Tibia-specific identity data.

  • Discord-native events, support, staff and automation workflows.
  • Configurable identity and progression concepts for other games.
  • Modular panels instead of a Tibia-only command structure.
  • Free path for smaller communities and evaluation.
  • Premium path planned for growing guild operations.

Why Premium will exist

Higher service levels should pay for real operational value.

Premium is intended for guilds that need longer metadata retention, broader limits, faster or more frequent managed refreshes, priority support and advanced automation. Exact differences will be published only after infrastructure and support costs are measured honestly.
