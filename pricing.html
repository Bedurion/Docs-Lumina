Founder Edition

Exclusive to Lumina.

Guild access only

Founder is the complete private edition created for Lumina and reserved exclusively for people who are accepted into our guild.

It cannot be purchased, unlocked through a Premium subscription or installed by another community. Founder access is a membership benefit connected to participation in Lumina on Secura and our official Discord server.

How to access it

Become part of Lumina.

  1. 01
    Apply to the guild

    Introduce yourself and submit your application through the official Lumina channels.

  2. 02
    Be accepted into our ranks

    Founder is available only after the guild accepts you as a genuine Lumina member.

  3. 03
    Join the verified ecosystem

    Register your character, connect your Discord identity and use Founder inside Lumina's live server.

What Founder means

Our living edition.

Founder is where Luminox operates at full depth under real guild activity. New systems are shaped, validated and refined here before selected capabilities are adapted for Community or Universal editions.

  • Private: built around Lumina's real structure and daily operations.
  • Complete: the broadest Luminox experience available within our guild.
  • Earned: access comes through accepted membership—not payment.
  • Ongoing: access depends on remaining an eligible member of Lumina.

Before you choose

Simple answers, no pressure.

Can we begin with Free?

Yes. Free is a functional starting point designed to prove value before a paid tier is needed.

Will upgrading reset our server?

No. The plan changes available capacity; it should not force staff to rebuild the configuration.

Where is Premium paid?

Core, Growth and Scale use a monthly subscription through Discord's integrated payment system.

Which tier should we choose?

Choose the smallest tier that removes today's limit. Growth is the balanced route for most active communities.
