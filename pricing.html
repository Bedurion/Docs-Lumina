Before you choose

Simple answers, no pressure.

Understand editions, billing and purchase protection before starting a subscription.

Can we begin with Free?

Yes. Free is a functional starting point designed to prove value before a paid tier is needed.

Will upgrading reset our server?

No. The plan changes available capacity; it should not force staff to rebuild the configuration.

Where is Premium paid?

Core, Growth and Scale use a monthly subscription through Discord's integrated payment system.

Is there a refund route?

Discord currently accepts Premium App subscription refund requests within five days of the initial purchase, subject to its case-by-case review.
