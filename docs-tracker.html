Detection model

Each outcome has a different meaning

ChangeMarkerImportant alert
Name change(changed)No defeat alert. The Tracker logs it and linked lists are renamed.
Traded character(traded)Enemy Defeated
Transferred away(transferred)Enemy Defeated with origin and destination worlds.
Traded and transferred(traded + transferred)One combined defeat alert, not two duplicates.
Returned(returned)Enemy Returned and reactivation in the configured world.
Deleted characterArchived as deletedEnemy Defeated after two confirmed not-found checks.
Disbanded guildArchived as disbandedEnemy Guild Defeated after two confirmed checks.
A rename is not a defeat

Renaming only updates identity references and creates a historical Tracker entry.

Blacklist organization

Records move instead of disappearing

  1. Blacklist · Disbanded / Deleted keeps removed historical entities.
  2. Blacklist · Out of This World keeps transferred characters outside the configured world.
  3. Blacklist · On This World contains active local threats and panel controls.

Characters outside the world remain in Blacklist history but are excluded from Enemies Online. If they return, they move back automatically.

Reliable delivery

Failed important alerts are retried safely

  • Alerts enter a small persistent outbox before sending.
  • Failed deliveries are retried for up to 24 hours.
  • Each alert carries a deterministic delivery marker to prevent duplicates after restarts.
  • An empty outbox makes no additional Discord or Tibia network requests.
  • Tracker lookups already required by the identity scan are reused.
