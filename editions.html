Choose Your Edition

The short answer

Choose by context, not by size.

Your edition determines what Luminox understands about your community. Your plan is a separate choice that determines available functions, limits, history and supported refresh speed.

Shared Luminox core

Both editions live completely inside Discord.

Administrators configure channels, roles and limits. Luminox then publishes guided panels with buttons, selectors, forms, private responses and threads for members and staff.

  • Panel-first
  • Free to start
  • Premium available
  • No external dashboard

Choose your context

Two clear paths.

You do not need technical knowledge to decide. Start with the type of community you manage and the information you want Luminox to understand.

Game-independent

Universal Edition

For any gaming guild, clan, creator community, roleplay group or organized Discord server that wants better workflows without tying its identity to one game.

Choose Universal when…
  • Your community plays any game—or several games.
  • You need general Events, Support, Loyalty, staff and moderation workflows.
  • You want Luminox to remain independent from game websites and character data.

Purpose-built for Tibia

Community Edition

For guilds and communities built around Tibia, the long-running MMORPG, with systems that understand characters, vocations, levels, worlds, guild membership and live game information.

Choose Community when…
  • Your server belongs to a Tibia guild or Tibia community.
  • You want verified characters, world and guild-aware identity or live online information.
  • You need specialized systems such as Loot Split, GuildBank, Tracker, Guards or Recruitment.

Decide in seconds

One question is usually enough.

If you want Luminox to understand Tibia itself, choose Community. If you want organized Discord workflows that stay independent from any particular game, choose Universal.

Playing Tibia but only need generic tools?You may still choose Universal. Community becomes the better fit when you want character, world, guild or live Tibia integrations.
Not sure how much capacity you need?Choose the edition first and begin with Free. You can evaluate Premium plans separately.

A third edition with a different purpose

Founder belongs only to Lumina.

Founder is the complete private Luminox environment built, used and refined inside our Tibia guild on Secura. It combines every eligible system, the deepest configuration and Lumina-exclusive workflows without public commercial caps.

It is not a Premium tier and cannot be purchased or installed by another server. Access is a benefit for people accepted into Lumina and connected to our verified Discord ecosystem.
