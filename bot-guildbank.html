In plain English

The balance only changes after the right confirmation.

Members can request a deposit from the permanent panel. Authorized staff can also create withdrawals. Each flow asks for the target account, character context, amount, source and note before showing a final confirmation.

Pending entries are not counted in the confirmed balance. Administrators always review; moderators need both a configured moderator role and the separate Treasurer role.

Three layers

Requests, review and history stay visually separate.

Create request

The guided panel validates the registered target and numeric amount.

Accumulate

Small eligible deposits can wait until the configured review threshold is reached.

Review

A Treasurer approves the request or denies it with a recorded reason.

Publish log

Reviewed movements remain above the control panel as compact permanent entries.

Safe corrections

One record controls its connected effects.

Approval

Confirmed deposits update the balance and can grant the configured Loyalty amount.

Denial

Denied requests preserve who reviewed them and why they were rejected.

Clean

Administrators can erase an incorrect record and reverse Loyalty tied to that exact entry.

Retention

Expired pending work becomes a final status while old metadata follows sustainable cleanup rules.
