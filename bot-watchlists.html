In plain English

A current list with a matching channel name.

Luminox reads the configured Tibia world, separates registered guild members from tracked enemies and publishes the result in the correct Discord panels.

The number in the channel name is updated from the same final dataset used to render the list. That prevents the panel from saying one online while the channel still says two.
