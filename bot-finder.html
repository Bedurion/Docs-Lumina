Bot · Team discovery

Turn online members into playable teams.

Finder compares current online characters, vocations, levels and member preferences to suggest compatible Tibia groups. Members can also mark themselves as Looking for team when they are ready to play.

In plain English

See who can actually play together.

A normal online list tells you who is connected. Finder goes one step further: it considers vocation, level compatibility and team-size rules to surface useful group suggestions.

Members can also join the Looking for team now board. The board appears at both the beginning and end of the list so nobody needs to search through a long channel.
