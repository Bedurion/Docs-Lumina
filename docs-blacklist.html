Documentation · Blacklist

Connect Tibia risk records to live guild intelligence.

Blacklist is a Community Edition system for exact Tibia characters and guilds. One reviewed record can drive Enemies Online, Identity Tracker, Guards, recruitment exclusions and permanent historical outcomes.

Add records

Characters and guilds use separate guided forms

  1. Choose Add character or Add guild. The panel opens the correct private modal.
  2. Enter the exact Tibia name. Exact spelling keeps later Tracker and online matching reliable.
  3. Assign Tibia Coins. Use zero when the record has no bounty.
  4. Write the reason. The public panel keeps the decision understandable.
  5. Review the refresh. The new record enters the correct active or historical section.
One source of truth

Do not maintain a second enemy list manually. Enemies Online and Guards read from these reviewed Blacklist records.

Edit or resolve

Update the existing record from selectors

  1. Open the Blacklist manager from the panel.
  2. Select Characters or Guilds.
  3. Select the exact entry; long lists expose Previous and Next pages.
  4. Edit the reason, edit the Tibia Coin amount, remove the record or mark its bounty paid.

Bounty paid resolves the active Blacklist decision through an explicit staff action. It does not silently delete the historical moderation context.

Connected systems

Every integration reuses the same reviewed identity

Enemies Online

Displays current-world blacklisted characters and blacklisted-guild online totals with Power and bounty context.

Identity Tracker

Detects supported renames, trades, transfers, returns, deletions and guild disbands.

Guards

Only eligible blacklisted targets can become enemy or battle reports; zero-TC targets cannot start a battle report.

Recruitment

Potential-recruit results exclude characters already present in Blacklist or Ban List.
