Shared data

One online snapshot powers multiple features

Members Online, Enemies Online, Finder, playtime, event attendance and session statistics reuse compatible cached data. A panel refresh does not imply a new request for every displayed character.

Result

Fast Founder Edition panels without multiplying Tibia requests as new visualizations are added.

Storage strategy

Keep balances; bound detailed history

  • Permanent account balances are stored independently from expiring reward detail.
  • Temporary states such as Looking for team, Guard duty and active reports expire automatically.
  • Support and event conversations remain in Discord archived threads.
  • GuildBank and recruitment keep the metadata required to audit and reverse rewards safely.
  • Daily backups retain 30 copies; monthly backups retain 12.
