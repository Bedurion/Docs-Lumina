Documentation · How it works

Collect useful information once. Reuse it across the guild.

Buttons and forms are handled immediately inside Discord. External game information is checked on a schedule and safely reused by every panel that needs it, avoiding unnecessary repeated requests.

Simple flow

Member action, system check, useful Discord result

1. A person acts

They use a command, panel, button, selector or form.

2. Luminox applies the rules

The relevant character, event, Loyalty, bank or staff system checks the request.

3. Shared information is reused

Guild settings, registered identities and current online data are read once where possible.

4. Discord shows the result

The bot updates a panel, counter, thread, log or private response.

Shared data

One online snapshot powers multiple Community features

In Community and Founder editions, Members Online, Enemies Online, Finder, playtime, event attendance and session statistics reuse compatible cached Tibia data. A panel refresh does not imply a new request for every displayed character.

Result

Fast live panels without multiplying Tibia requests as new visualizations are added.

Storage

Keep important totals; clean temporary detail

  • Account balances remain even after old reward details are cleaned.
  • Looking for team, Guard duty and active reports remove themselves after their useful period.
  • Support and event conversations remain in archived Discord threads.
  • GuildBank and recruitment keep enough information to review or reverse a reward correctly.
  • Guildhall keeps current rooms and occupants while permanent decisions remain visible in Discord logs.
  • Daily backups retain 30 copies; monthly backups retain 12.

These are service-policy intervals, not a promise that every system performs an external request on every cycle. Immediate actions can update a panel sooner. If Tibia or Discord temporarily fails, the last valid result remains visible instead of being replaced by an empty panel.

Editions

One foundation, three different purposes

Universal EditionGame-independent Discord operations such as Support, general Events, moderation automation, Loyalty and staff workflows.
Community EditionAdds verified Tibia guild identity, characters, progression, specialized events, economy and live intelligence.
Founder EditionLumina's private complete edition with every feature enabled, no commercial caps and the shortest safe service schedules.

Changing a public edition or plan changes capability and service policy without deleting the server's existing configuration. Founder activation remains restricted to the authorized Lumina server.
