Questions?

Documentation · FAQ

Quick answers for common Luminox questions.

Short explanations for registration, roles, panels, data, refreshes and Discord limitations.

Why must I register a character?

Registration proves ownership and lets Luminox sync roles, nicknames, events and leaderboards.

Can I register characters from another world?

No. The configured guild world controls what characters can register in that Discord server.

Do points disappear if I change main?

No. Loyalty and GuildBank totals belong to the Discord account, not the character.

Why can’t Luminox change the owner nickname?

Discord does not allow bots to change the server owner nickname.

Where do closed support tickets go?

They are locked and archived Discord threads, accessible through archived threads or My Tickets.

Does Finder make extra Tibia requests?

No. Finder uses cached online data already collected by Luminox.
