Lumina

Guild · Community

Organized gameplay without losing the social side of a guild.

Lumina uses structure to reduce confusion, not to make the community feel mechanical. Clear systems create more time for playing, helping and talking together.

Group activities

Members organize hunts, quests, bosses and flexible events through dedicated boards with clear participants and discussion threads.

Visible progression

Levels, playtime, streaming, Loyalty and contribution panels make activity visible without turning guild life into an obligation.

Private help

Registration issues, complaints and sensitive questions can move into private role-aware support threads.

Shared intelligence

Online lists, Finder, guards and tracker alerts give members useful context without filling general chat with repeated requests.

Multiple languages

International members can participate while Discord timestamps and structured panels reduce ambiguity around dates and workflows.

Accountability

Verified identities and audit logs help staff make decisions from clear records rather than rumors or forgotten conversations.

Discord matters

One shared place for the information that affects everyone.

Discord is where Lumina publishes rules, claims, event boards, GuildBank records, support and guild alerts. Registration connects that community identity to the member's Tibia characters and current rank.

The objective is not to replace spontaneous conversation. It is to keep important information available after the conversation moves on.

Community standard

Ask early, communicate clearly

If a claim, event, registration or member issue is unclear, contact staff before the situation becomes a conflict. The Support panel exists so asking for help is private and traceable.
