Give authority only where responsibility exists.

Luminox supports multiple moderator roles, separate specialist roles and structured staff decisions so one broad permission does not unlock every operational panel.

In plain English

Moderator is the base. Specialist is the job.

A server can configure more than one global moderator role. Sensitive modules then require a second role for the relevant responsibility, such as Treasurer, Recruiter, Guildhall staff or a category-specific Support role.

Discord administrators always retain oversight. A moderator only receives the additional control when both the global and specialist requirements are satisfied.

Structured decisions

Applications and votes use the same identity rules.

Private staff nominations

Eligible staff can nominate a registered guild member and vote without exposing who initiated or supported the candidate.

Public applications

Members submit their own main character, contribution, availability and staff experience to a public candidacy panel.

Private applications

Members who prefer privacy can continue through the configured Support ticket channel.

Administrator conclusion

Administrators archive a vote or invite the selected candidate to accept or decline privately.
