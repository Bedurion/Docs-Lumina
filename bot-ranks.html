Bot · Guild identity

Let Tibia promotions update Discord access.

Community Edition maps verified Tibia guild ranks to managed Discord roles, follows promotions and demotions automatically and gives registered outsiders a separate Guest role without touching unrelated server roles.

In plain English

The guild website becomes the authority for guild access.

Administrators connect each Tibia rank to the Discord role that represents it. When Luminox confirms a member's current rank, it removes the other managed rank roles and applies the correct one.

A registered character outside the configured guild can keep a Guest role instead. That allows guests to preserve verified identity without receiving member channels or guild authority.
