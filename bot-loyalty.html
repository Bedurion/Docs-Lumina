In plain English

A transparent guild progression system.

Your guild decides which actions deserve recognition. Luminox records eligible rewards from events, GuildBank contributions, recruitment, server boosts, streaming, staff support and other configured activity.

Points belong to the Discord account, not one character. The member's public level and private profile therefore stay consistent even when they use several registered characters.

Why it stays fair

Every important change has context.

Eligibility rules

Rewards only apply when the account satisfies the configured membership requirements.

Reversible rewards

Linked actions can remove their own points when a transaction is erased or a protected recruit leaves early.

Account-wide levels

One level follows the Discord account and can appear beside eligible characters.

Manual corrections

Administrators can add or remove points with a reason that remains visible in the history.
