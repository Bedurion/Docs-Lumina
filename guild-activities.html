From idea to memory

Activities have a clear beginning and a lasting result.

Luminox keeps the practical parts organized so the group can focus on the activity itself. Members see what is happening, choose whether to join and use the event thread to coordinate.

  1. 01

    Someone opens the activity

    The organizer explains the objective, requirements and whether the schedule is fixed or open to voting.

  2. 02

    Members take their places

    Participants join with eligible characters while the board keeps the group and reserves understandable.

  3. 03

    The thread carries the plan

    Changes, questions and coordination remain connected to the event instead of disappearing in general chat.

  4. 04

    The result becomes history

    Completed activities can contribute to Loyalty, progression and the visual or written guild archive.
