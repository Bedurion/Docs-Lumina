Board setup

Assign each event type independently

Hunts/events set hunts channel:#hunts
Bosses/events set bosses channel:#bosses
Quests/events set quests channel:#quests
General events/events set general channel:#events

The board remains the final channel message and lists links to every active event. Channel names display the active count.

Creation suite

Click Create and choose how scheduling works

  1. Open the correct board and click Create.
  2. Choose Set date now, Vote · Leadership decides or Vote · Automatic.
  3. Enter title and description. Automatic voting also requires the expected duration.
  4. Choose reservation/team composition for hunts or minimum participants for every other type.
  5. Review the event character. Your registered main is selected by default, but you can choose another compatible character.
  6. Review the result and publish.

A timezone is required before event creation. Natural inputs such as “tomorrow 20:30”, “in 3 hours” and localized equivalents are converted from the creator's timezone into Discord timestamps.

Schedule voting

Propose ranges and choose manual or guarded automatic scheduling

  • Leadership and every joined participant can submit up to three proposals.
  • Leave the date blank to mean every day.
  • Use a fixed date, a weekday, every Friday or localized equivalents.
  • Use one time such as 17:00 or a range such as 17:00-20:00.
  • Each participant may vote for multiple proposals, including proposals created by other members.
  • Future participants can vote after joining; votes from members who leave or are kicked stop counting.
  • Discord timestamps convert exact and next recurring occurrences for every viewer's timezone.

Manual mode: Leadership selects any proposal or publishes a different final date.

Automatic mode: Luminox acts only after every active participant has voted, the required team is present, one option wins without a tie and that time range covers the full event. The winning author must also be able to receive Leadership safely.

For hunts, Main and Reserve positions are reordered by availability. If any safety check fails, nothing changes and Leadership can finish the vote manually.

Joining

Registered characters and contextual reactions

  • The first join asks which registered character to use.
  • Members can save “always use my main” and disable it later.
  • Hunt reactions validate vocation and shared-experience range.
  • Quest and general Event accept every vocation.
  • Leaving or being kicked remains crossed out in the event record with time and responsible user.
  • Every participant gains access to the event discussion thread.

Leadership

The creator joins immediately and controls the event

Leadership can close/reopen signups, edit, kick members and transfer leadership. The new leadership receives those permissions and the previous one loses them. Hunt leadership transfer is blocked when the new level would invalidate shared experience for active members.

Delete Event is available to leadership, moderators and administrators and requires confirmation. Schedule changes and cancellations notify participants inside the thread.

Automatic completion

No manual success-vote buttons

At the official end, Luminox checks the configured minimum or required team and uses online attendance already collected by the guild systems. If the requirements are met, the event becomes successful automatically.

Successful leadershipAdditional 100 Loyalty Points.

Expiration and archive

Panels disappear; Discord keeps the thread history

  1. At the end time, status becomes Expired and both panel and thread receive the [X] prefix.
  2. The expired panel remains visible for 24 hours but no longer counts as active.
  3. Luminox posts a final detailed summary in the thread.
  4. After the visibility period, the event panel is deleted.
  5. The thread is locked and archived in Discord instead of being deleted.

This preserves discussion history without filling the bot database with thread messages or attachments.
