Pricing · Guarantee

Know your options before subscribing.

Luminox Premium uses Discord's native subscription system, including Discord's official billing support and refund-review process.

5days

Official request window

Request through Discord.

Discord's current policy allows a refund request for a Premium App Subscription within five days of the initial subscription purchase.

Clear, not misleading

This is a review window—not an automatic refund promise.

Discord reviews refund requests case by case and determines eligibility at its sole discretion. Luminox does not override Discord's payment decision or describe the five-day route as a no-questions-asked money-back guarantee.

Official policy controlsDiscord may update its billing rules. The policy shown by Discord when you purchase or request support always takes precedence over this summary.

Refund request

Four steps, handled in Discord.

Submit the request early and include the information Discord asks for before the eligibility window closes.

01
Check the purchase date

Confirm that the initial Premium App subscription purchase is within Discord's current five-day request window.

02
Find the payment ID

Open Discord User Settings and Billing on desktop or browser, or use the purchase confirmation email where applicable.

03
Contact Discord Billing

Use the email associated with the Discord account and select the Premium Apps billing category.

04

Discord confirms the outcome after reviewing the request under its current Refund Policy.

Who handles what?

Payment protection stays with the payment platform.

This separation keeps the process transparent and avoids asking a buyer to send sensitive payment details through a guild channel.

Discord

Billing decision

  • Processes the Premium App subscription.
  • Stores the relevant payment record.
  • Reviews refund eligibility.
  • Handles cancellation and plan changes.
Luminox

Product delivery

  • Provides the plan capabilities described at purchase.
  • Preserves server configuration during eligible plan changes.
  • Explains features, limits and setup clearly.
  • Provides a route for product-support questions.

Important details

Know the exceptions before checkout.

Initial purchase

The standard five-day Premium App request window applies to the initial subscription purchase described by Discord's current policy.

Case-by-case review

A request within the window is not automatic approval. Discord determines eligibility and generally limits refunds by purchase category.

iOS purchases

Refunds for Premium App purchases made through Apple's iOS payment flow are handled through Apple under the applicable App Store process.

App removal

Discord's policy also describes a possible prorated request when an eligible Premium App is removed from the server, submitted within five days of that event.
