Meet the bot

Luminox organizes guild work inside Discord.

Luminox is a Discord-native, panel-first guild bot. Administrators use a small set of setup commands to connect channels, roles and limits; members and staff then run daily workflows through buttons, selectors, guided forms and private threads without leaving Discord.

How Luminox works

Configure once. Publish a panel. Let members click.

There is no separate Luminox dashboard to host, open or teach. A panel is a persistent bot message placed in a normal Discord channel, complete with buttons, selectors, instructions and private responses. Administrators configure the foundation; the community operates the resulting system visually.

Live panels show the latest state and refresh when relevant data changes. Permanent Discord messages preserve reviews, decisions and historical records above the final control panel.

1 · InstallAdd Luminox to Discord and choose Universal or Community Edition.
2 · ConfigureAdministrators connect the channels, roles and limits required by one function.
3 · PublishLuminox posts the guided panel where members already communicate.
4 · OperateMembers and staff click controls while panels, logs and threads stay in Discord.
Commands are not the daily interface

Slash commands remain mainly for installation, configuration, private overviews and deliberate maintenance. A small number of personal utilities remain available, but recurring workflows such as Registration, Events, Loot, Support and moderation-list management are panel-driven.

Thread-native organization

Ongoing work gains its own place without filling the channel.

When a workflow needs conversation, Luminox keeps the permanent launcher in its configured channel and opens a linked Discord thread for the active case. The channel list shows what is happening at a glance, while the detailed discussion remains attached to the correct ticket, event or submission.

  • Support opens a private thread for the requester, invited participants and responsible staff.
  • Events keep planning, schedule changes, mentions and the final summary inside the activity thread.
  • Reports and submissions can use the same pattern when a review needs focused discussion.
  • Closing a workflow locks or archives its thread, so Discord preserves the history without Luminox copying every message into a second database.

Which edition?

Universal runs general Discord workflows. Community understands Tibia.

Choose Universal for game-independent Events, Support, Loyalty, moderation and administration. Choose Community when your guild needs verified characters, Tibia world rules, live online data, GuildBank, Tracker, Guards or Recruitment.

  • Both editions begin with a Free plan.
  • Premium unlocks more functions, capacity, history and faster supported refresh policies.
  • Founder Edition is the unrestricted private build used inside Lumina.
