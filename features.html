Meet the bot

Luminox organizes guild work inside Discord.

Luminox is a Discord bot made of optional functions. Administrators configure only what their guild needs, and members use clear buttons and forms while the bot keeps panels and permanent records up to date.

How Luminox works

Configure once. Publish a panel. Let members click.

Luminox is a Discord bot, not a separate dashboard your guild must learn. Administrators run the setup commands; everyday users interact with the panels that appear in normal Discord channels.

1 · InstallAdd Luminox to Discord and choose Universal or Community Edition.
2 · ConfigureSelect the channels, roles and limits required by one function.
3 · PublishLuminox posts a permanent panel with buttons, selectors and instructions.
4 · OperateCurrent panels refresh while permanent logs remain readable above them.

Which edition?

Universal runs general Discord workflows. Community understands Tibia.

Choose Universal for game-independent Events, Support, Loyalty, moderation and administration. Choose Community when your guild needs verified characters, Tibia world rules, live online data, GuildBank, Tracker, Guards or Recruitment.

  • Both editions begin with a Free plan.
  • Premium unlocks more functions, capacity, history and faster supported refresh policies.
  • Founder Edition is the unrestricted private build used inside Lumina.
