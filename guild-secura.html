How we want to be known

Reputation is built one interaction at a time.

The guild name follows members outside our Discord. Our conduct on Secura should make that name easier to trust.

01

Respect existing systems

Follow the current Secura booking rules, communicate about claims and avoid turning a scheduling issue into personal conflict.

02

Represent the guild responsibly

Scams, abuse, repeated toxicity and deliberate disruption affect more than one character. They affect the community behind the name.

03

Resolve problems with context

Use evidence and private support when a situation needs staff attention instead of escalating through public accusations.

04

Make room for cooperation

Share information, create activities and help other members participate in content they could not easily organize alone.

Why world verification matters

The server configuration protects a coherent guild identity.

Luminox knows which world the Discord server represents. Character registration accepts eligible characters from that configured world, verifies ownership through Tibia.com and keeps guild roles connected to the member's real in-game status.

  • A verified character proves account ownership without asking for Tibia credentials.
  • Guild rank synchronization follows the official Lumina roster.
  • Characters outside Lumina can remain registered as guests when the server allows it.
  • World restrictions prevent unrelated characters from receiving misleading access.
