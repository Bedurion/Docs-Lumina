02Terms of ServiceThe conditions for visiting the website, installing Luminox and using its Discord panels.+

1. Acceptance and scope

By visiting GuildLumina.com, installing Luminox, configuring it in a Discord server or using its panels, you agree to these Terms. If you act for a Discord server or organization, you confirm that you have authority to accept these Terms and configure the requested permissions for that server.

2. Eligibility and account responsibility

You must satisfy Discord's minimum age and local legal requirements. If you are below the age of legal majority, a parent or legal guardian must review any paid-service decision where required. You are responsible for protecting your Discord account and for activity performed through it.

3. What Luminox provides

Luminox is a Discord-native guild and community management bot. Administrators use setup commands to connect roles, channels and enabled functions; members then use persistent panels, buttons, selectors, modals and private Discord threads. Available functions depend on edition, plan, server configuration and third-party platform availability.

4. Server administrator responsibilities

  • Grant only the Discord permissions required by enabled functions and keep the bot role in an appropriate position.
  • Configure moderator and specialist roles accurately and review access after role changes.
  • Tell members which rules, retention periods and moderation procedures apply in the server.
  • Keep server data, descriptions and instructions accurate and avoid using Luminox for unlawful surveillance or harassment.

5. Acceptable use

You must not misuse Luminox or GuildLumina.com. Prohibited conduct includes attempting unauthorized access, bypassing plan or permission controls, exploiting rate limits, distributing malware, submitting illegal or infringing content, harassing users, impersonating staff, manipulating rewards or records, or using automated traffic that harms the service or third parties.

6. Moderation, suspension and termination

Server administrators control their Discord communities subject to Discord's rules. Lumina may restrict or terminate access to Luminox where necessary to protect security, comply with law, respond to abuse, enforce these Terms or protect other users. Where practical, the affected server will receive an explanation and a reasonable route to contact us.

7. Availability and changes

We work to keep Luminox reliable, but cannot promise uninterrupted or error-free service. Discord outages, API limits, Tibia website availability, hosting incidents and maintenance can delay panels or external checks. We may change, replace or retire functions when required for security, platform compatibility, legal compliance or product development.

8. Disclaimers and liability

Luminox supports guild decisions but does not replace human review. Administrators remain responsible for sanctions, financial records, recruitment decisions and permissions in their own server. To the maximum extent permitted by law, Lumina is not liable for indirect or consequential loss, loss caused by third-party platforms, user-provided data, unauthorized server configuration or use contrary to these Terms. Nothing here excludes rights or liability that cannot legally be excluded.

9. Governing law and disputes

These Terms are governed by Spanish law, without removing mandatory consumer protections available in the user's country of residence. Please contact us first so we can attempt a practical resolution. Consumers in the European Union retain all non-waivable statutory rights and access to competent authorities and courts.

10. Changes to these Terms

Material changes will be dated on this page and communicated through an appropriate public or Discord channel where practical. Continued use after an effective change means acceptance of the revised Terms, except where law requires separate consent.

03Privacy PolicyWhat information Luminox processes, why it is needed, where it lives and your rights.+

This Privacy Policy explains how Lumina processes personal data through GuildLumina.com and Luminox. For these activities, Lumina Guild acts as the service controller unless a server administrator independently determines purposes and means for data inside that server.

1. Data we may process

Discord identityUser, guild, channel, role, message and interaction identifiers; display names; role membership; and server configuration needed to run selected functions.
Game identityPublic Tibia character name, world, guild, vocation, level and verification relationship where Community Edition registration is used.
Operational recordsPanel state, event participation, Loyalty transactions, GuildBank records, recruitment reviews, moderation actions, boost or stream eligibility and support-ticket metadata.
Community contentText, descriptions, credits and approved media submitted for the public blog or gallery.
Technical dataSecurity and diagnostic logs, timestamps, error context and GitHub Pages visitor IP logging performed for platform security.

2. Why we process it

  • Provide configured Luminox functions and keep Discord panels and permissions operational.
  • Verify Tibia character ownership and apply guild/world rules where enabled.
  • Maintain transparent records, prevent duplicate rewards, investigate abuse and secure the service.
  • Answer support, legal and privacy requests.
  • Review and publish content that the contributor has submitted for public display.
  • Manage Free or Premium entitlement status received from Discord without handling payment-card details.

3. Legal bases

Depending on the activity, processing relies on performance of the requested service or contract, legitimate interests in operating and securing a transparent community service, consent for optional public content or other optional activities, and compliance with legal obligations. Consent can be withdrawn for future processing without affecting prior lawful processing.

4. Where information is stored and shared

Data is shared only where needed with service providers and platforms involved in operation. Discord stores interactions, threads and attachments under its own policies. GitHub Pages hosts this static website and may log visitor IP addresses for security. Tibia public pages provide public character and guild information. Infrastructure providers may process limited technical information to host, secure or back up Luminox.

No payment-card storage

Lumina and Luminox do not need to receive or store your full payment-card details. Discord and its payment providers control the checkout and subscription transaction.

5. International transfers

Discord, GitHub and other providers may process information outside the European Economic Area. Their own privacy statements describe the safeguards used for international transfers. We select established providers and limit the information shared to what the service requires.

6. Retention

We keep data only while it remains necessary for the relevant function, security, accountability or legal obligation. Configuration and linked identities remain while the server or account uses the service. Temporary interactions and detailed history follow feature- and plan-specific cleanup rules. Support and event conversations can remain archived in Discord. Approved public content remains until hidden, replaced or removed. Security records may be retained for a limited period needed to investigate incidents.

7. Automated processing

Luminox uses rules to calculate eligibility, refresh panels, expire temporary states, detect configured identity changes and allocate transparent rewards. These rules do not make decisions that produce legal or similarly significant effects without an available human review route. Sensitive guild actions remain subject to server staff authority.

8. Your rights

Where the GDPR applies, you may request access, rectification, erasure, restriction, data portability or object to processing based on legitimate interests. You may withdraw consent at any time for future optional processing. Use the official Contact page and identify the Discord account, server and request. We may need to verify identity before disclosing or changing data.

You may also lodge a complaint with the Spanish Data Protection Agency (AEPD) or the competent supervisory authority in your country.

9. Children and minors

Luminox is not directed at children below Discord's permitted age. Server administrators must operate age-appropriate communities and must not knowingly use Luminox to collect unnecessary information from minors.

3. Review and editorial control

Submission does not guarantee publication. Authorized staff may correct formatting, request changes, reject, hide or remove content to protect privacy, quality, safety, legal compliance and the guild's editorial standards. Material edits should preserve the contributor's meaning; staff may return an article to its author for revision.

Published material may show the agreed display name, character name, category, date and credit. Public pages can be indexed, copied, cached or shared by search engines and third parties. Contributors should submit only material they are comfortable making public.

5. Removal and complaints

Discord remains the review workspace

06Paid Plans, Billing & RefundsHow Discord-managed Premium subscriptions, renewals, cancellations and refund requests work.+

1. Free and Premium access

Luminox can offer Free access and paid Premium tiers. The selected edition determines the type of functions available; the selected plan determines eligible features, limits, update policy and retention. The current Pricing page and Discord checkout show the offer presented before purchase.

2. Discord-managed checkout

Premium App subscriptions are purchased and managed through Discord's official payment flow. Discord or its payment processor controls payment authorization, stored payment methods, regional currency, applicable taxes and transaction receipts. The final amount shown by Discord at confirmation is authoritative.

3. Renewals and cancellation

Unless the checkout states otherwise, subscriptions renew according to the billing period displayed by Discord. You can cancel through Discord's subscription controls. Cancellation normally prevents the next renewal while access continues until the current paid period ends, subject to Discord's current terms.

4. Plan changes

Upgrades, downgrades and their effective dates are handled through Discord. Discord may apply prorated charges for an upgrade or schedule a downgrade for the next billing period. Review the confirmation screen before accepting a plan change.

5. Refund requests

Discord's current refund guidance states that a Premium App Subscription refund request may be submitted within five days of the initial purchase and is reviewed case by case. A request is not an automatic guarantee. Mandatory consumer withdrawal, conformity and refund rights available under applicable law remain unaffected.

6. Access restrictions

A refund, charge reversal, expired subscription, downgrade or serious breach of these Terms can remove Premium entitlement. Server configuration should remain recognizable where practical, but features or capacity above the active plan may stop operating until eligible access returns.

Payment privacy

Lumina receives only the entitlement and limited transaction context made available by Discord for operating the subscription. It does not need your full card number or payment credentials.
