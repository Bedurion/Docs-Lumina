In plain English

Two reports for two different jobs.

Report enemy shares a blacklisted character's current location and notifies the configured alert role. Report battle does the same, but also activates guard duty when necessary and starts measuring battle participation for the selected registered character.

Both reports open a Discord thread, expire after inactivity and can be extended from the thread. Guard duty itself lasts up to five hours unless the member disables it sooner.
