Features · Support

Give members a private support path without filling public channels.

One clean panel routes each request into a private Discord thread visible only to its creator, invited participants and the staff responsible for that category.

Role-aware routing

The right request reaches the right people.

Administrators define support categories, descriptions, required questions, up to five responsible staff roles and an optional minimum user role. A category can therefore be open to everyone, restricted to members or routed to a specialist team.

Responsible staff need a global moderator role plus one role assigned to the category. Discord administrators retain complete oversight.

Complete staff workflow

Responsibility stays visible from opening to archive.

Claim ownership

One staff member can claim the ticket, transfer the claim or release it while administrators retain oversight.

Invite participants

Authorized staff can add another member or specialist to the private thread and remove them before closure.

Member resolution

The creator can mark the issue resolved; staff can close when the workflow is complete.

Satisfaction follow-up

The creator can rate the result after closure. No response after three days counts as satisfactory.

Staff Loyalty

Meaningful participation, ticket closure and satisfactory support can reward eligible staff under documented rules.

History stays in Discord

Closing locks and archives the private thread immediately. Messages and images remain in Discord instead of being copied into a second transcript database.

Easy navigation

My Tickets works for members and staff.

A private response with page buttons lists tickets by latest activity and identifies whether the viewer created the ticket or participated as staff. Active and archived threads remain reachable without exposing them in the public Support channel.

Ticket-list history follows the configured edition: Founder can preserve it indefinitely, Premium keeps it for ten years and Free keeps it for one year. The full conversation remains in the archived Discord thread while Discord keeps it available.
