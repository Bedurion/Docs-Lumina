Bot · Community Finder

Help compatible Tibia players find each other while they are ready to play.

Finder turns current Tibia online information already collected by Community Edition into ranked team suggestions and gives members a temporary, visible way to announce what they want to do.

Useful without extra load

Match from data Luminox already has.

Finder reuses online state, level, vocation, session duration and activity already collected for the guild watchlist. It scores shared-experience compatibility, vocation balance, level spread and online context without starting another Tibia request cycle.

  • Duo suggestions can include any compatible two vocations.
  • Groups of three or more require tank and healer balance.
  • Each character appears on its own readable line.
  • Long results split before Discord limits are reached.

Looking for team

Intent matters as much as online status.

A member can choose any registered character — even while offline — add an optional description and remain visible from one to five hours. Account-level exponential cooldowns discourage repeated toggling.

Fast opt-in

Choose a character or save the main as the default for future Looking for team actions.

Useful context

Add the activity or team type being sought. Without a description, Finder displays “Any team”.

Automatic expiry

Temporary state is removed when its selected duration ends instead of filling long-term storage.

Immediate refresh

Adding or leaving Looking for team redraws the panel immediately and updates the channel counter.

Match history

A private paginated response preserves recent compatible groups with clickable character links.

Navigation

The Looking for team block appears at both ends of long Finder output with top and action links.

Turn online time into groups

Give members a reason to check one channel before asking everywhere.

Finder stays compact, current and connected to the same identities already used across Luminox.
