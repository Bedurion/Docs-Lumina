Respect first. Clear communication always.

These principles summarize how Lumina expects members to behave. The complete current rules published in Discord remain authoritative.

Open official rulesJoining process

Respect claims

Follow the Secura booking system, guild claims and staff decisions. Do not turn a misunderstanding into harassment or endless kill stealing.

Use your real identity

Register your characters honestly. Do not use alternate accounts, deleted registrations or another person's character to bypass restrictions.

No abuse

Toxicity, scams, power abuse, repeated disrespect and deliberate damage to the guild can lead to sanctions.

Communicate in Discord

Read announcements, use the correct panels and keep your registration available so ranks and important information remain accurate.

Support the team

Respect event commitments, communicate if plans change and help create a guild where members can rely on one another.

Ask staff early

Use private Support when evidence, complaints or personal situations should not become a public argument.

Discord rules are the source of truth

This website explains the guild publicly, but rules can evolve. Always consult the linked Discord rules before applying or acting on a sensitive situation.
