In plain English

Write locally. Display globally.

A member chooses their timezone once. When Luminox creates a native Discord timestamp, Discord itself displays the correct local date and hour to every reader.

For ordinary chat messages containing a recognizable time, the optional clock reaction can also compare the author's timezone with the reader's timezone and return a short-lived conversion.
