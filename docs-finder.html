Panel setup

How to configure the Finder panel

Admins assign the Finder channel with /set finder channel:#finder. The panel uses cached online data to show matches and looking-for-team entries.

Command/set finder channel:#channel
PublishesFinder matches, Looking for team controls and Match history button.
RequiresGuild and online tracking systems configured so the panel has data to display.
RefreshUse /sync all or wait for automatic watchlist refresh.
