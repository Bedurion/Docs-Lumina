A transparent bank log for guild economy.

GuildBank keeps contributions, deposits, withdrawals and treasurer reviews visible in Discord.

Panel setup

How to configure the GuildBank control panel

Admins assign the GuildBank channel with /set guildbank log channel:#guildbank. That channel receives the bank control panel, compact log entries and pending review tools.

Log channel/set guildbank log channel:#channel
Treasurer role/set guildbank treasurer-role role:@Role. Admins can review by default; moderators need this role.
Minimums/set guildbank minimum amount:number review-amount:number controls accumulation and review threshold.
Manual actionsUse panel buttons for Deposit, Withdraw and Clean, or command fallbacks when needed.
