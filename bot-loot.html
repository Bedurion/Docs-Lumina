In plain English

No spreadsheet and no hidden arithmetic.

The member clicks Split loot, pastes the analyzer and answers a short sequence of questions about market values, the looter and any chosen compensation.

Luminox returns a readable result with final balances and transfers. If someone contributes part of their share, that amount can accumulate in the same GuildBank review process instead of becoming an unrelated note.
