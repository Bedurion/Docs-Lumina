Panel setup

Publish one compact final message

/loot set channel:#loot

The panel contains Split loot and How it works. Member-facing /loot is retired; the panel opens the same complete workflow.

Guided workflow

From analyzer text to transfers

  1. Click Split loot.
  2. Paste the complete Tibia Party Hunt Analyzer session.
  3. Select Market or the supported valuation mode.
  4. Identify the looter and any compensation preference.
  5. Choose whether part of the split contributes to GuildBank.
  6. Review the generated transfers and confirm the input.

Calculation model

Every participant receives an equal final share

Luminox uses each participant's loot, supplies and balance. The resulting transfer list tells the looter or responsible payers exactly what to send so all final balances match the selected rules.

Example

If one member looted most of the session value while another paid most supplies, the first member transfers the difference required to equalize their final share.

GuildBank contribution

The reserved share is distributed equally

When enabled, every participant contributes equally to the reserved GuildBank amount, including the looter. Only registered eligible Discord accounts can receive contribution history and Loyalty credit.

Small shares are accumulated per account according to the GuildBank configuration. Once the account reaches the review threshold, authorized staff can confirm the contribution.

Transfer confirmer

One responsible account at a time

  • The user who starts the split is the initial transfer confirmer.
  • Only the current confirmer can confirm completion.
  • Change confirmer transfers that responsibility to another participant.
  • The previous confirmer immediately loses confirmation permission.
  • The public message updates to show the current responsible account.

Safety and logs

Review before trusting the result

  • Use complete analyzer text with correct participant names.
  • Unregistered characters can participate in the split, but cannot receive Discord GuildBank/Loyalty accounting.
  • GuildBank operations remain pending until the configured review conditions are met.
  • Staff review records the responsible accounts and source Loot message link.
  • Use the panel's How it works response for an in-Discord summary.
