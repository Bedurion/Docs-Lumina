Before you begin

Five words to know.

These terms appear throughout the guides. Understanding them once makes the rest of the documentation much easier.

Luminox
The Discord bot described on this website.
Panel
A bot message with buttons or selectors that members can use.
Module
One feature area, such as Registration, Events, GuildBank or Support.
Log
A readable record of important actions kept in a Discord channel.
Edition
The product version: Universal for gaming communities or Community for Tibia guilds.
