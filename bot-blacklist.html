In plain English

Record the decision once. Reuse it everywhere.

Authorized staff add an exact Tibia character or guild, explain the reason and assign a bounty. Luminox then reuses that same record wherever current enemy context is needed.

The panel separates active targets on the configured world, transferred targets outside it and resolved or deleted history, so old decisions remain available without polluting live lists.

Community Edition

Purpose-built for Tibia rather than generalized moderation.

Blacklist depends on Tibia characters, guilds, worlds, vocations, levels and ownership changes. It is therefore exclusive to Community Edition, available from the Core tier and included in Founder.

Universal Edition uses the separate Ban List for game-independent Discord sanctions.
