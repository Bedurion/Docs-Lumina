Private staff voting

Anonymous internal candidacies

/moderators set voting channel:#staff-voting
  • Only staff can start a vote or vote.
  • The candidate is selected through their registered main character.
  • Existing staff cannot be nominated again.
  • One Discord account can vote once for each candidate.
  • The initiator and individual voters remain private.
  • Administrators archive the candidacy or invite the selected candidate.

Public applications

Members apply with context, not an empty vote card

/moderators set applications channel:#applications

The public panel lets eligible guild members submit their registered main character and allows guild voting. Every application must explain what the candidate can contribute, their staff experience in this or other games, their game experience and the time they can commit — including an indefinite commitment when appropriate.

A second button routes members who prefer privacy to the configured Support channel instead of publishing their application.

Administrators

Administrator is determined by Discord

Luminox does not maintain a separate administrator role list. A member is an administrator when Discord grants the Administrator permission. Administrators can bypass specialist-role requirements where documented, but their actions are still logged.
