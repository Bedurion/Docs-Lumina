One economic chain

From hunt analyzer to confirmed GuildBank history.

Members paste their Tibia hunt analyzer into the Loot Split panel, choose the looter and complete the guided questions. Contributions below the configured review threshold accumulate safely on the target account instead of disappearing.

Direct deposits and withdrawals follow the same account-linked record model. Administrators and moderators with the additional Treasurer role can approve or deny pending entries.

  • Configurable minimum recorded deposit and review threshold.
  • Pending totals excluded from confirmed balance.
  • Compact permanent logs after review.
  • Loyalty awarded only when the eligible contribution is approved.

Built-in controls

Accountability at every step.

Guided split

Market and leader logic, looter selection, compensation choices and a final confirmation before publishing.

Specialist review

Moderators need both a global moderator role and the configured Treasurer role. Discord administrators retain oversight.

Approve or deny

Deny with Not found, Amount mismatch or a custom reason. The result remains part of the audit history.

Clean corrections

Administrator cleanup removes an erroneous bank record and reverses Loyalty linked specifically to that record.

Sustainable history

Expired requests and old transaction detail are cleaned while confirmed balances and final Discord logs remain.

Readable numbers

Large amounts use separators consistently, making deposits, balances and rewards easy to verify on desktop and mobile.
