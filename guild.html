Who we are

Friendly enough to feel human. Structured enough to work.

We play on Secura and welcome members who value respect, communication and long-term guild life. Discord registration is mandatory because it connects the person, their Tibia characters and the guild access they receive.

Luminox grew from that need. Instead of depending on disconnected bots and manual lists, Lumina built one system for registration, ranks, events, economy, Loyalty, support and intelligence.

Our priorities

A stronger guild starts with consistent expectations.

Respect claims

Bookings, hunting places and community agreements matter. Conflict should be solved through evidence and staff support, not escalation.

Use Discord

Registration, announcements, events, support and guild decisions live in Discord so members share one reliable source of information.

Play together

Individual progress matters, but the guild becomes valuable through hunts, bosses, quests, streams and members helping one another.
