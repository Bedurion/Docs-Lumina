Start with the basics

Lumina is a player guild inside Tibia.

Tibia is a persistent fantasy MMORPG—a massively multiplayer online role-playing game—first released in 1997. Players develop characters, explore a shared world and cooperate or compete with other people online.

Lumina is one organized group within that game. Our characters play on the world Secura, our official roster exists on Tibia.com and our international community uses Discord to remain connected outside the game client.

What the guild feels like

Friendly enough to feel human. Structured enough to last.

Systems reduce confusion, but people remain the point. Lumina uses clear expectations and shared tools so members can spend more time playing, helping and building memories together.

Play together

Hunts, bosses, quests, events and roleplay.

Members can join organized objectives, create their own activities or simply find others who want to share the same part of Tibia.

Explore guild activities →
Protect the name

Reputation matters on a shared world.

Claims, agreements and respectful conflict resolution shape how Lumina is experienced by members and the wider Secura community.

Ask for help

Private support when a public channel is not enough.

Registration issues, complaints and sensitive questions can move into role-aware private Discord threads.

Build a history

Contribution becomes part of a lasting profile.

Events, Loyalty, GuildBank activity, streaming and other contributions make long-term participation visible without replacing the social side of the guild.
