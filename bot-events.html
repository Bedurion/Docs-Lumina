In plain English

One event becomes one clear source of truth.

The organizer clicks Create, answers the guided questions and publishes an event card. Members join from reactions or controls on that card instead of building an unreliable list in chat.

Universal Edition supports general Discord events. Community Edition adds Tibia hunts, bosses and quests with character identity, vocation slots, party composition and game-aware attendance.

Planned together

Invite a guildmate before someone else takes the place.

On character-based Community and Founder boards, the creator can select another Discord member and one of that account's eligible registered guild characters during the final review. Current Leadership, administrators and configured moderators can add more participants after publication.

The invited character appears with an Invited status, follows the same Main or Reserve order as a normal signup, receives thread access and can leave without staff intervention.

  • Guild membership and registration remain mandatory.
  • Hunt vocation and shared-experience rules still apply.
  • Invited participants count in schedule voting and automatic team checks.
  • A kicked account cannot be silently added back to the same event.
