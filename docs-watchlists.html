Live online awareness for members and enemies.

Watchlists use Tibia online data to show who is online, session time and recent changes.

Panel setup

How to configure watchlist panels

Admins assign watchlist channels with /set watchlist. Different watchlist types can use different channels.

Command/set watchlist type:... channel:#channel
Members onlineShows guild members online, session time and recent level gains.
Enemies onlineShows followed enemies and blacklisted guild online summaries.
Manual entriesUse /watchlist add, /watchlist edit and /watchlist remove.

Members online

Guild activity

Shows online guild characters, vocation, level, session duration and level gains.

Enemies online

Risk awareness

Enemies can be followed manually or through blacklist integration.

Blacklist and guilds

Separate but connected

  • Blacklist has its own panel.
  • Blacklisted characters appear in enemies.
  • Blacklisted guilds can show online counts.

Name and world changes
