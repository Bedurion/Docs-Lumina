Why Lumina built it

Publishing stays beside the community that creates the content.

There is no external editorial dashboard for members to learn. Finished Gallery proposals use a guided panel, while longer Blog stories are written naturally across one or several messages in a private Discord thread.

Authors revise drafts before review. Authorized staff then approve, edit, hide, restore or remove live Blog articles from the original thread, preserving one understandable editorial history inside Discord.

Security boundary

The bot does not push arbitrary files into the website.

Allowlisted media

Gallery accepts one validated JPG, PNG or short MP4 file; Blog accepts up to four JPG or PNG images. Executables and unsupported formats are rejected.

Private review

The proposal thread is visible only to its creator and responsible staff.

Validated artifact

Approval produces controlled metadata rather than executing or trusting the original attachment.

Separate credentials

Repository publication happens outside the bot process with narrowly scoped access.
