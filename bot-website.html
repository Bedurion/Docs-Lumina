In plain English

Discord holds the review process. A separate publisher updates the site.

Members submit finished Gallery media through a guided form or write Blog articles naturally inside private Discord threads. Authors can revise drafts before review while authorized staff approve, edit, hide, restore or remove live Blog articles from the original thread.

Approved Blog articles publish automatically. Gallery proposals use the same staff review, but a Discord administrator launches their final publication. Luminox never executes an attachment and never gives a Discord user arbitrary access to the website repository.

Security boundary

The bot does not push arbitrary files into the website.

Allowlisted media

Gallery accepts one validated JPG, PNG or short MP4 file; Blog accepts up to four JPG or PNG images. Executables and unsupported formats are rejected.

Private review

The proposal thread is visible only to its creator and responsible staff.

Validated artifact

Approval produces controlled metadata rather than executing or trusting the original attachment.

Separate credentials

Repository publication happens outside the bot process with narrowly scoped access.
