Designed for real moderation

The panel answers the questions that matter.

Who is restricted?

Use a Discord member in either public edition. Community can additionally identify an exact Tibia character.

Why was it applied?

A written reason remains beside the target instead of being lost in private staff conversation.

How long does it last?

Choose a duration or keep the sanction unlimited, then revise that decision later from the manager.

Who made the decision?

The responsible Discord staff account and recorded time remain visible for accountability.
