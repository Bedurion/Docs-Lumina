In plain English

A living room directory inside Discord.

Authorized staff configure whether the guild owns a Guildhall, its name and up to one hundred rooms. Every room can have a name, description, size in square metres, quality order and current occupant.

Members claim a room with a registered main or alternate character. Staff approve, deny, move or remove occupants, and every important change becomes a permanent log above the current panel.

Who can act?

Members request. Specialists decide.

Guild member

Browse and claim with a registered character.

The member sees ordered rooms, chooses an available option and submits the eligible character who would occupy it.

Guildhall staff

Configure rooms and review occupancy.

Administrators always qualify. Moderators also need the separately configured Guildhall specialist role before they can approve or edit rooms.
