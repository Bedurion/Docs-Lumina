Flexible scheduling

Set the date now or let participants find the best window.

Leadership can publish an exact date immediately, open a vote that leadership concludes, or enable guarded automatic scheduling. Participants propose fixed dates, weekdays, recurring days, exact times or ranges in their own timezone.

Automatic mode only acts when the event has enough active participants, everyone has voted, one option wins without a tie, the duration fits the winning availability and Leadership can be transferred safely.

Automatic success

Use objective rules, not a manual popularity vote.

At the official end, Luminox checks the selected composition or minimum. Community Tibia events can validate online attendance from shared current data; Universal general events use their Discord participant list. When Loyalty is unlocked, qualifying participants receive 50 points and successful Leadership receives an additional 100.

  • Created events award 10 points when Loyalty is available and the event reaches its scheduled end.
  • Community attendance requires repeated observations and a meaningful part of the event duration.
  • Unavailable attendance data does not unfairly fail an otherwise valid event.
  • Schedule changes and cancellations notify participants inside the thread.
