Flexible scheduling

Set the date now or let participants find the best window.

Leadership can publish an exact date immediately, open a vote that leadership concludes, or enable guarded automatic scheduling. Participants propose fixed dates, weekdays, recurring days, exact times or ranges in their own timezone.

Automatic mode only acts when the event has enough active participants, everyone has voted, one option wins without a tie, the duration fits the winning availability and Leadership can be transferred safely.

Purpose-built boards

The same lifecycle, the right rules for each activity.

Hunts

Duo, trio, Team 4, Team 5 and unrestricted compositions with vocation slots, Main/Reserve order and Tibia shared-experience validation.

Bosses

Tanks, healers and DPS stay separated by role and ordered by arrival without a one-per-vocation restriction.

Quests

Any vocation can join. Leadership defines the minimum number of participants required for success.

General events

A flexible any-vocation format for activities that do not fit hunts, bosses or quests.

Leadership controls

Edit, close signups, kick, transfer Leadership, schedule recruiting events or delete after a confirmation step.

History stays in Discord

Expired panels disappear after 24 hours while the final summary and discussion remain in a locked archived thread.

Automatic success

Reward participation from objective rules, not a manual popularity vote.

At the official end, Luminox checks the selected composition or minimum and validates online attendance from data already collected by the guild systems. A qualifying participant receives 50 Loyalty Points; successful Leadership receives an additional 100.

  • Created events still award 10 points when they complete their lifecycle.
  • Attendance requires repeated observations and a meaningful part of the event duration.
  • Unavailable attendance data does not unfairly fail an otherwise valid event.
  • Schedule changes and cancellations notify participants inside the thread.
