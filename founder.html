Founder questions

The private edition, explained plainly.

These answers separate Founder membership access from the public Luminox editions and Premium plans.

Can another server purchase Founder Edition?

No. Founder cannot be purchased, subscribed to or installed by another community. Universal and Community are the public editions.

Is Founder a fourth Premium tier?

No. Free, Core, Growth and Scale are public service plans. Founder is a private edition attached to Lumina membership, not a billing tier.

Does every Discord visitor receive Founder access?

No. Joining the Discord server is not the same as being accepted into Lumina. Access follows verified membership and the responsibilities assigned to each role.

Does Founder include every future feature automatically?

Founder is where the complete Lumina environment is operated and new work is validated, but every feature still needs safe implementation, correct permissions and a real guild use case.

Does “no public caps” remove Discord limits?

No. Founder removes Luminox’s public commercial plan caps. Discord component, message, upload, rate and permission limits still apply.

What happens if someone is no longer an eligible Lumina member?

Founder access follows current verified membership. Luminox reconciles managed roles and active eligibility while preserving the history that must remain auditable.
