How Luminox works

Register once. Use that identity everywhere.

After a member verifies a character, Luminox can connect the correct Discord account, nickname, guild role and main character. Events, GuildBank, Loyalty and live lists can then use the same verified profile instead of asking for the same details again.

  • Guild and world restrictions protect identity quality.
  • Role mappings follow the member's verified main character.
  • One current online check powers watchlists, Finder and attendance.
  • Discord threads preserve conversations without duplicating attachments in the bot database.
