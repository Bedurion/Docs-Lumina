One connected guild OS

A member registers once. The rest of the server becomes smarter.

Registration gives Luminox the verified identity needed to synchronize nicknames and ranks, authorize event participation, attribute GuildBank activity, calculate Loyalty and make every list more useful. Systems share trusted data instead of asking members to repeat it.

  • Guild and world restrictions protect identity quality.
  • Role mappings follow the member's verified main character.
  • One current online check powers watchlists, Finder and attendance.
  • Discord threads preserve conversations without duplicating attachments in the bot database.
