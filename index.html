Our Tibia journey

A guild built to progress together on Secura.

Lumina brings international players together in one Tibia world. We organize hunts, quests, bosses, guild events and roleplay while building a community where members can develop their characters without playing alone.

  • Verified membership connects each Discord account to real Tibia characters.
  • Clear rules and leadership protect respectful coordination.
  • Shared activities create progression, stories and long-term friendships.
  • Discord keeps an international guild connected across different timezones.

Born inside the guild

Meet Luminox, the bot behind Lumina.

We built Luminox to solve real problems in our own Discord. Administrators publish each system with a short setup command; members and staff then use guided panels, buttons, selectors and private threads directly inside Discord. Other guilds can install that same panel-first experience—without learning an external dashboard or memorizing operational commands.
