In plain English

Choose where protection applies and keep the result explainable.

Automod is enabled channel by channel. It detects normal and commonly disguised links, watches the prohibited language configured for that channel and applies the selected isolation period only when the author lacks permission to bypass the rule.

The permanent moderation log is a separate destination for registrations, managed role transitions, guild departures and staff actions. It remains useful even when the live panel later changes.
