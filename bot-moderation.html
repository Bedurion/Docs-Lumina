Community restriction behavior

Registration remains; managed guild access is removed.

Keep verified history

Adding a registered account to Blacklist or Ban List does not erase its character ownership record.

Remove guild access

Luminox removes only the roles it manages for guild membership while preserving unrelated Discord roles.

Use Guest correctly

A restricted account with registered characters can remain as Guest; no registrations means only everyone remains.

Lift restrictions

Authorized buttons remove a character or account from the list and allow normal synchronization again.

Permanent accountability

Every staff change says what, who and when.

Blacklist, Ban List and role changes publish linked, readable log entries. Historical messages remain fixed; current panels refresh in place and include navigation to the log.
