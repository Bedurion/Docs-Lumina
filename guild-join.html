Joining process

From interested Tibia player to verified Lumina member.

You apply for the in-game guild through our Discord. Registration confirms which Secura character belongs to your Discord account; it does not automatically accept the application.

Apply in Discord

Join Lumina in Tibia

After acceptance and in-game admission, Luminox detects your Lumina rank and grants the matching Discord guild access.

Character verification

Your Discord identity is connected to your Tibia character.

The Registration panel asks for the exact name of a character on Secura and gives you a temporary verification code. Place that code at the beginning of the character comment on Tibia.com, then use Register character again with the same name.

  • Characters from Antica or another Tibia world cannot register in this Discord while Secura is configured.
  • Registration proves ownership of the character; it does not mean the character has already joined Lumina.
  • An accepted Lumina member receives the Discord role mapped to their current in-game guild rank.
  • A verified Secura character outside Lumina may use configured Guest areas while getting to know the community.
  • Timezone setup is included in the first registration and remains saved if character verification needs another attempt.
