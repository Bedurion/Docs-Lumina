In plain English

A leaderboard is a view, not another data collector.

Luminox builds rankings from information its systems already maintain: character levels, deaths, Loyalty, GuildBank contribution, played time, streamed time and guard battle participation.

Long rankings are split into multiple Discord messages. The first message acts as the index and later pages include working navigation back to the top.

Examples

Different rankings answer different questions.

Character progress

Top level, rolling level gains, deaths and played time show in-game activity.

Account contribution

Loyalty and GuildBank rankings follow the registered Discord account.

Community activity

Total streamed hours recognizes members who share gameplay in Discord.

Guard participation

Accumulated battle time shows how characters contributed against tracked enemies.

Position movement

Movement needs two comparable snapshots.

A new installation begins with a baseline, so existing entries initially show no movement. Later refreshes compare the previous ranking with the new ranking and display only genuine position changes.
