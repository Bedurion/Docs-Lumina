In plain English

Every active boost runs its own verified cycle.

A member can contribute one boost or several. Luminox keeps those units separate, shows the next expected reward and grants 5,000 Loyalty Points per boost after every completed 30 days of uninterrupted support.

If one boost ends before its current period is complete, only that unfinished progress is cancelled. Rewards from periods that already completed remain in the account history.
